# 4. HOW TO TALK ABOUT THE PRODUCT


## Direct Response Copywriting & Content Guide

The secret to converting prospects into sales is not hard-selling or spamming links. It is positioning yourself as an advisor who understands their exact struggle.


## Different Marketing Angles to Use


### Angle 1: The Library Marathon Trap

> "Studying 8 hours a day is not a badge of honor if you forget 80% of it in the exam hall. Here is the active recall system top students use instead..."


### Angle 2: The Carryover & GPA Rescue Angle

> "Retaking a failed course costs time, tuition, and embarrassment. Preventing a carryover with a N5,000 proven study framework is the smartest investment you can make this semester."


### Angle 3: The Busy Student-Entrepreneur Angle

> "You do not have to choose between making money and getting good grades. Here is how to structure your 24 hours so both thrive."


## Curiosity Hooks Library

* "Why reading your lecture notes 5 times is actually destroying your exam score (and what to do instead)."
* "The 90-minute study rule used by first-class students to retain 3x more information."
* "How to stop freezing in the exam hall when you see unexpected questions."

## Social Media & WhatsApp Status Sequence Example

<b>Frame 1 (Pain Point):</b> "Hate the feeling of reading all night only to stare at an exam paper blankly?"

<b>Frame 2 (Insight):</b> "It is not your memory. It is your study method. Rote memorization fails under exam stress. Active recall never does."

<b>Frame 3 (Solution + Call to Action):</b> "The Academic Comeback Package details the exact step-by-step active recall blueprint. Click the link to grab your bundle now."
