# 7. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


### Q1: Who is the package for?

It is for any student in tertiary institutions (university, polytechnic, college) who wants to improve their study efficiency, raise their GPA, and conquer exam anxiety.


### Q2: How is the product delivered? Is it instant access?

Yes! Access is 100% instant. As soon as the customer completes payment, they are immediately redirected to their digital library and an email with access credentials is sent to their inbox.


### Q3: What if a customer wants a refund?

The refund is 100% backed by the creator. Customers simply reach out to support. As an affiliate, you never bear refund costs, and earned commissions are not deducted.


### Q4: How and when do affiliates get paid?

Affiliate payouts are sent twice a month (Mid-month and End-of-month) directly to the bank account listed in your affiliate dashboard.


### Q5: Can I promote on WhatsApp, Telegram, or social media?

Yes! WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and X (Twitter) are highly recommended channels.


### Q6: Can I run paid ads?

Yes, paid advertising (Meta Ads, Google Search, TikTok Ads) is permitted as long as guidelines and ethical promises are respected.
