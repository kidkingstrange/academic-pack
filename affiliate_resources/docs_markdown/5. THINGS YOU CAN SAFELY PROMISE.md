# 5. THINGS YOU CAN SAFELY PROMISE


## The Creator Money-Back Guarantee (Critical Information)

> Customers are protected by a 100% Money-Back Guarantee provided directly by the product owner.<br/><br/>• If a customer is genuinely unsatisfied, the refund is handled and guaranteed 100% by the creator.<br/>• The affiliate NEVER bears the cost of refunds.<br/>• Legitimate customer refunds DO NOT reduce, deduct, or cancel your earned affiliate commission balance.<br/>• You can confidently mention this guarantee in all your promotion!


## Suggested Refund Wording You Can Copy

> "If you don't find the Academic Comeback Package valuable, your money will be refunded. The guarantee is provided directly by the creator, so you can purchase with total confidence."


## What You Can Safely Promise

* Instant digital delivery to all 7 books and study frameworks immediately after payment.
* Access to proven active recall and exam preparation blueprints.
* Practical step-by-step guides on time management, focus, and exam question decoding.

## What You MUST NOT Promise (Forbidden Statements)

* <b>DO NOT</b> promise specific letter grades or GPAs (e.g., "Guaranteed 4.5 GPA without studying").
* <b>DO NOT</b> promise that buying the book alone without applying the steps will pass their exams.
* <b>DO NOT</b> create fake scarcity timers or misleading countdown claims on personal chats.
