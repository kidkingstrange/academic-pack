# 6. AFFILIATE GUIDELINES


## Partnership Terms & Operational Guidelines


### Commission Structure

Affiliates earn a generous <b>50% commission</b> on every successful customer sale generated through their unique referral link or referral code (N2,500 per N5,000 sale).


### Sales Tracking Mechanism

Sales are tracked seamlessly via browser cookie attribution and referral code locking at checkout. When a customer clicks your link, your attribution is locked to their transaction.


### Payout Schedule & Processing

Affiliate payout balances are processed <b>twice every month</b>:

* <b>Mid-Month Payout:</b> Covers earnings accrued in the first half of the month.
* <b>End-of-Month Payout:</b> Covers earnings accrued in the second half of the month.
Payouts are transferred directly into your registered Nigerian bank account (Access, GTBank, Kuda, Zenith, OPay, Palmpay, UBA, etc.) as logged in your affiliate dashboard.


### Expected Professional Conduct

* No spamming in official university group chats or unapproved forums.
* Maintain high ethical standards and represent the brand with excellence.
